# More SlashBlade Ex Enchantment Effects

An addon mod for SlashBlade: Resharped that greatly expands the enchantment system of Slash Blades, giving each sword a
unique combat style.

# Introduction

SlashBlade: Resharped's original enchantment enhancement system only includes Soul Speed and Feather Falling, which is
clearly insufficient on the path of pursuing ultimate combat in the Shura realms.

This mod adds a large number of additional enchantment enhancement effects to Slash Blades, allowing them to use almost
every enchantment that exists in vanilla Minecraft. From armor-type enchantments that enhance defense, to weapon-type
enchantments that change the performance of shooting arts, to special enchantments adapted to specific
environmentsâ€”every sword swing will be filled with infinite possibilities.

Through carefully combining these enchantment enhancement effects, you can create the perfect sword tailored to your own
combat style. Whether you're a Summoned Sword master focused on ranged combat or a sword art expert proficient in
close-quarters combat, you'll find the most suitable enchantment combination for yourself.

# Feature List

This mod **does not** have a configuration file, but you can prevent specific enchantments from being applied to Slash
Blades through the configuration file of its prerequisite mod: mrqx's Slashblade Core.

## Armor-type Enchantment Enhancements

- Protection, Blast Protection, Projectile Protection, Feather Falling, Respiration, Frost Walker, Swift Sneak, Aqua
  Affinity, Depth Strider, and other armor enchantments can now be applied to Slash Blades.
- The effects of these enchantments are the same as on armor and can stack with similar effects from other sources.

## Summoned Sword Quantity & Quality

- **Multishot**: Increases the number of Summoned Swords fired in all shooting arts, without consuming additional Proud
  Soul Value.
- **Piercing**: Summoned Swords can penetrate multiple targets, each level increases maximum penetration count.
- **Infinity**: Using basic Summoned Swords no longer consumes Proud Soul Value.

## Summoned Sword Enhancement

- **Quick Charge**: Reduces charging time for Summoned Sword Arrays, 3 ticks reduction per level.
- **Punch**: Summoned Swords launch targets upward upon hit, each level increases launch force.
- **Flame**: Summoned Swords have a chance to become fire swords, with increased probability in hot environments.
- **Channeling**: Summoned Swords have a chance to call lightning, with significantly increased probability during
  thunderstorms.

## Environmental Adaptation

- **Impaling**: Deals extra damage to targets in water/rain, +20% damage per level.
- **Riptide**: When swinging blade in water/rain, propels you forward and increases damage, +20% damage and speed per
  level.
- **Lure**: Pulls targets closer when hitting with blade swing, each level increases pull strength.
- **Luck of the Sea**: When killing creatures in water/rain, adds Luck of the Sea level to Looting level.

## Other Utility Enhancements

- **Efficiency**: Blade swings have a chance to spawn an extra Summoned Sword and break shields.
- **Loyalty**: When performing Proud Soul Enchanting or copying SA/SE, has a chance to not consume materials.
- **Silk Touch**: Doubles success rate for Proud Soul Enchanting. When copying SE, can preserve the SE on the blade.
